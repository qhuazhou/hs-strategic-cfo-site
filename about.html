About the practice

Development finance and financial coordination for projects that need to keep moving.

HS Strategic CFO Advisory connects project models, funding applications, draws, progress claims, financial reporting, and stakeholder requirements—alongside strategic CFO, real estate, and transaction advice.

What the practice combines

Development finance, project reporting, funding and draw workflows, financial modelling, real estate analysis, and CFO-level operating discipline in one advisory process.

Project financeFunding applicationsDraw managementFinancial reporting

Advisory approach

Make the financial work usable. Models, budgets, applications, claims, reports, and stakeholder information should reconcile and support the next project decision.

What this means for clients

You get a model, application, claim package, report, or advisory process that connects the numbers to the actual project decision and deadline.

  • If project costs, funding, and cash requirements do not reconcile, the reporting should show where and why.
  • If a draw or funding application needs information from several consultants, the workflow should make ownership and deadlines clear.
  • If a project or business needs CFO support, the work should clarify cash, risk, reporting, and the next financial decision.
