About the practice

Integrated finance, real estate, tax, and transaction advisory for decisions that need to hold up.

HS Strategic CFO Advisory is built for clients who need integrated advice, not fragmented opinions from separate tax, finance, real estate, and business advisors.

What the practice combines

Real estate market analysis, tax-aware structuring, valuation, financing, and CFO-level operating discipline in one advisory process.

Advisory approach

Integration over coordination. Tax, valuation, financing, cash flow, market analysis, and deal structure are not separate problems. They are one decision seen from different angles.

What this means for clients

You get a decision memo, model, or advisory process that connects the numbers to the actual choice in front of you.

  • If the property does not work after tax and refinancing, the model should say so.
  • If an exit valuation expectation is unrealistic, it is better to learn before negotiation.
  • If a business needs CFO support, the work should clarify cash, tax, reporting, and the next financial decision.
