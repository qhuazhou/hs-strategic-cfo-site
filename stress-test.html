Real Estate Investment Stress Test

A 5-minute test to see whether a property still works after tax, debt, vacancy, repairs, refinancing pressure, ownership structure, documentation, concentration, and exit costs.

Answer seven questions. See where the investment may be exposed.

This is not a tax return, appraisal, or formal valuation. It is an initial review that helps identify whether the numbers deserve a closer look.

What the test checks

Ownership structure, true cash flow, interest-rate stress, tax exposure, exit path, documentation, and concentration risk.

After the test

If the result is yellow or red, the next step is a focused Real Estate Investment Review: a 60-90 minute review, risk score, and concise decision memo.
